PRIVACY POLICY

Sift Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sift handles information when you use the Sift app and this website.

Overview

Sift is designed to keep your task information close to you. Based on the current app implementation, task data and app preferences are stored on your device rather than in a Sift-operated cloud service.

Information the app may access

Sift may request access to the following, only when relevant to app features you use:

  • Your task entries, deadlines, saved places, and app settings.
  • Your location, if you enable location-based reminders.
  • Notifications, if you allow reminder alerts.
  • Your microphone and speech recognition permissions, if you use voice input.
  • Purchase status information from Apple for in-app purchases.

How information is used

  • Task entries, saved places, and settings are used to power the core task management experience.
  • Location data is used to support optional geofenced reminders and directions-related flows.
  • Notification permission is used to deliver due date and location reminders you choose to enable.
  • Voice input is used to transcribe spoken text into task content.
  • Purchase data is used to determine access to paid features.

Data storage

Based on the current app code, Sift stores task data, saved places, and preference settings locally on your device. We do not currently operate a Sift backend that stores your task list for you through this app version.

Speech recognition

Sift uses Apple speech recognition frameworks for voice input. When on-device recognition is available, the app requests it. Depending on device capabilities and Apple platform behavior, speech processing may still involve Apple services. Please review Apple’s privacy materials for additional details about how Apple handles speech and dictation features.

Location services

If you enable location features, Sift uses location information to refresh your current location, support location-linked tasks, and trigger geofence reminders. You can disable location access through your device settings at any time.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Sift does not receive your full payment card details. Apple may share transaction or entitlement information needed to confirm your purchase status.

Sharing

We do not currently describe any Sift-operated advertising, analytics, or data-broker sharing in this website policy for the present app implementation. If product behavior changes, this policy should be updated before those changes ship.

Your choices

  • You can choose whether to grant location, microphone, speech recognition, and notification permissions.
  • You can remove app data from your device by deleting tasks in the app or uninstalling the app, subject to how your device manages local storage and backups.

Children’s privacy

Sift is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children through a Sift-operated online service in its current form.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, support, feedback, or bug reports, contact siftapp.contact@gmail.com or use the feedback & bug report form.
